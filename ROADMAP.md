# PassTheCert — Strategic Roadmap

> Strategy and post-MVP vision. For execution status see `PROJECT_STATUS.md`.
>
> North star metric: % of users who pass their certification after using PassTheCert.

---

## Positioning

**One-liner:** "The fastest way to know if you're ready to pass. Not a game. A system."

**Competitive edge:** Real spaced repetition (SM-2) + visible Readiness Score + frictionless diagnostic funnel. No competitor combines all three.

**Brand mascot:** The raven — "the bird that never forgets" — ties memory and intelligence to the SM-2 differentiator.

## Competitor map

| Competitor | Price | Strength | Our edge |
|---|---|---|---|
| SecuSpark | $8.33/mo | RPG gamification | Science over games |
| Pocket Prep | $21/mo | Polished UI, daily questions | Real SM-2, lower price |
| Mindirus | Freemium | 16 certs, 51K questions | Focused, better UX |
| ExamCompass | Free | Large question bank | Tracking + adaptive engine |

**Pricing anchor:** Pro launches at €14.99/mo (canonical price in `INSTRUCTIONS.md`).

---

## Phase 2 — Post-MVP

Ship only after Phase 1 (the live MVP) is stable.

- Expand question bank to 300+ Security+ questions (currently 287; 100+/domain target).
- Email sequences post-diagnostic to nurture unconverted leads.
- UX iterations driven by feedback from the first paying users.
- Pricing A/B test (€14.99 baseline vs higher tiers).
- SEO blog with Security+ content for organic traffic.
- AI Tutor (Claude API) for explanation enrichment.

## Phase 3 — Growth

- Second certification (CompTIA Network+ or AWS Cloud Practitioner).
- Mobile app or PWA optimization.
- Admin dashboard for question management.
- AI-assisted question generation with human review.
- Referral program.
- Team / enterprise plans.
